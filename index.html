Overview

Hailing from Princeton, our team designs, builds, and programs robots to play soccer in the national Robocup Junior competition. We are Jieruei Chang, Shrey Khetan, and Yangwenbo (William) Yao; specializing in software, electrical, and mechanical design respectively. Last year, we won second place in the US National competition, as well as the Best Engineering Design award.
